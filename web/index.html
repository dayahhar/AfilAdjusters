Industries Served

Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM)

BNM oversees financial stability and regulations in Malaysia. For adjusters and investigators, BNM provides critical information on financial regulations, insurance standards, and industry practices essential for compliance and effective operations.

Lembaga Hasil Dalam Negeri (LHDN)

LHDN manages tax collection and enforcement in Malaysia. Adjusters and investigators can benefit from LHDN's resources on tax regulations and compliance requirements, crucial for accurate financial reporting and investigations.

Kementerian Dalam Negeri (KDN)

KDN handles internal security and public safety. Adjusters and investigators can find valuable guidelines and regulatory updates related to security protocols, risk management, and investigative procedures.

Sistem Pengurusan Integrasi Kemahiran Malaysia (MySPIKE)

MySpike offers cutting-edge monitoring and data management solutions, crucial for insurance adjusters and investigators in Malaysia to enhance accuracy and efficiency in their claims and investigations processes.

Malaysia Insurance Institute (MII)

The Basic Certificate Course in Insurance Loss Adjusting (BCCILA) by the Malaysia Insurance Institute covers insurance principles, loss adjusting, legal frameworks, and ethics. It combines self-paced e-learning and virtual instructor-led training on motor and non-motor insurance, culminating in multiple-choice exams.
