Our History

AFIL ADJUSTERS & INVESTIGATORS SDN BHD (AFIL) is a one of the registered and licenced Insurance and Takaful Loss Adjusters in Malaysia. Currently, the directors are actively involved with its daily operation.

Founded in 1985, with office humbly started at Jalan Ipoh, we have settled in Wangsa Maju, Kuala Lumpur since 1996. We believed a strategic and permanent location is important to build a strong business network in providing immediate attendance for the adjusting work.

Over the years, we expanded our network throughout Malaysia in major cities such as Penang, Kuantan, Kota Bharu, Melaka, Johor Bahru, Kuching and Kota Kinabalu.


We earned our reputation through the integrity, diligence, equitable and fair assessment of claims and most of all the honesty of recruited staffs

We believed the highly trained and dedicated staffs will be our human capital that sustained our company in the competitive industry. The dedicated team has experience and skill in dealing with various types of insurance claims from inception to the final settlement through their abilities to communicate and negotiate with various parties in their own technical language.

In 2023, we managed to save insurer and takaful operators, 36 percent of own-damage claims cost from the repair estimate and had a TAT of 3.1 days (Improvement of 1% and 0.3 day compare to previous year)

Our Vision

AFIL shall sustain among the loss adjuster firms in Malaysia while keeping the environment safe to all our stakeholders and minimize the wastage in any form while carrying our duty.

Our Mission

We obliged to serve promptly, equitably, efficiently and always with integrity and honesty
